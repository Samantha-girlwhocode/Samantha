Our Story

Esperanza means hope in Spanish. Throughout 2020 I've found it's been especially important to hold onto hope that things could get better. With school going online I realized the majority of other high schoolers have been more discourged than ever to learn. Personally at first I felt the same but as quarentine dragged on I began coding and learning languages more than ever. Through doing so I developed certain methods of learning that make things more fun! Through being envolved in robotics and math team at school I saw the gender disparity in STEAM fields and overall lack of passion and so through "Esperanza" I hope to get more people, especially other girls around my age more excited about coding and languages!

Often learning seems boring or intimidating so an aspect of our method is trying to make it more fun and stress free by incorperating pets and making information from books easier to understand!
